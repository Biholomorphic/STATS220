Data Collected via Energy Drink Observations

This analysis is based on observations of energy drink presence collecected from the University of Auckland and Auckland University of Technology campuses. The data captures snapshots of individual brands observed at specific times and places.

Summary of Observations

Across 89 observations, we documented energy drink instances ranging from a minimum of 1 to a maximum of 4 drinks per location snapshot. The mean observed quantity was 1.55 drinks, with a median of 1, indicating that while most observations show relatively low quantities, some locations captured clusters of drinks.
